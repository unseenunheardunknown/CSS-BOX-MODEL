The Box Model

Margin

The margin is the amount of space between the border and the outside edge of the element.

The margin gives you the ability to move the content based on the top right left bottom values.

Example: of using property margin is using a rule set that looks like this in the css file. p { margin: 100px 70px 50px 25px;}

The content will move down 100px (margin-top), then move left 70 px (margin-right)

, then move up 50px (margin-bottom),and lastly move the content right 25px (margin-left) on the webpage.

Border

The border is the thinkness and style of the border surrounding the content area and padding.

Example: of border property being used is this; #Border{ border: 2px solid black }.

You can see the css ruleset of border being used right now on the webpage!

Take a look at the black outline of the box titled Border.

Padding

The padding is the amount of space between the content area and the border.

The way I like to think of padding.

Is that the padding expandes and contracts the content.
